Digitheque: Decentralized magazine platform for all

Aiming to make the internet more pleasant and accessible for those with something to say.

The Digitheque is an experiment in using older technology to solve recent problems.

We work off the following concepts:

  • The core internet is to be embraced.
  • Syndication is a foundation upon which to build.
  • Curation should lie in the hands of the beholder.

What is a decentralized magazine?

Imagine a local neighborhood newspaper, but every single person has a hand in writing the articles. The community as a group has a voice. We want to apply this principle to any sort of community.

The natural curation of the community voices resembles less of an 'internet forum' and looks more like traditional published media. Users can subscribe to particular members or explore the community hub.

We are in a very early stage of development, please contact me through my email found in the footer for inquiries and ideas.
